---
title: 'Roasted Salmon'
description: 'Simple oven-roasted salmon with lemon, garlic, and herbs.'
author: 'Zijin'
tags: ['seafood', 'salmon', 'dinner', 'healthy', 'easy']
source: 'https://www.lecremedelacrumb.com/best-easy-healthy-baked-salmon'
servings: '2-4 people'
prepTime: '10 minutes'
cookTime: '12-15 minutes'
totalTime: '25 minutes'
ingredients:
  - category: 'Salmon'
    items:
      - '4 fillets (about 6oz each)'
      - '2 tablespoon olive oil'
      - '½ teaspoon salt'
      - '¼ teaspoon black pepper'
  - category: 'Seasoning'
    items:
      - '2 cloves garlic, minced'
      - '1 lemon (zest + juice), plus wedges for serving'
      - '¼ teaspoon each of dried oregano, thyme, parsley, and basil'
instructions:
  - 'Heat oven to 400°F (220°C). Line a sheet pan with parchment (or lightly oil it).'
  - 'Pat salmon dry. Place on the pan and rub with olive oil.'
  - 'Season with salt and pepper.'
  - 'Mix together olive oil, juice from ½ lemon, and dry seasoning. Rub the mixture generously over the salmon.'
  - 'Roast 12–15 minutes, depending on thickness, until the salmon flakes easily and is just cooked through.'
