---
title: 'Oven Roasted Chicken Breast'
description: 'Juicy, tender, oven-roasted chicken breast with a simple spice rub.'
author: 'Zijin'
tags: ['chicken', 'dinner', 'high-protein', 'easy']
source: https://www.recipetineats.com/oven-baked-chicken-breast/#recipe

servings: '4 servings'
prepTime: '5 minutes'
cookTime: '18 minutes'
totalTime: '23 minutes'
ingredients:
  - category: 'Chicken'
    items:
      - '4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (5-6 oz each)'
      - '2 tsp olive oil'
  - category: 'Seasoning'
    items:
      - '1 1/2 tbsp brown sugar'
      - '1 tsp paprika'
      - '1 tsp dried oregano or thyme'
      - '1/4 tsp garlic powder'
      - '1/2 tsp each salt and pepper'

instructions:
  - 'Brine the chicken breast.'
  - 'Preheat oven to 400°F (convection).'
  - 'Mix seasoning.'
  - 'Dry the chicken breast; pound to an even thickness.'
  - 'Drizzle chicken with oil, then add the dry seasoning and rub well.'
  - 'Place chicken on a lined baking sheet.'
  - 'Bake for 18 minutes, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F.'
